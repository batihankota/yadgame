About the Game

1. Behind one of the three doors in the game is a brand new car, and behind the other two are goats.
2. The objective is to choose the door with the car behind it and win the car.
3. Before the contestant's chosen door is opened, the computer reveals one of the other doors behind which there is a goat.
4. At this point, two closed doors remain: the contestant's chosen door and the unchosen door.
5. The contestant is given the option to switch to the other remaining door.
6. The contestant will either stick with their initial choice or switch to the other door.
7. After the contestant's final choice of door is opened, it is revealed whether they have won the car or not.
